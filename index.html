Path Keeper Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July, 2026

Introduction

Path Keeper ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when using Path Keeper.

By using Path Keeper, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information

Path Keeper collects your precise location only when you start a tracking session.

Your location is used to record your route accurately, display your current position, generate route history, and support offline navigation features.

Path Keeper does not continuously track your location after you stop tracking.

Offline Map Data

Path Keeper downloads offline maps for the areas you use.

Offline maps are stored locally on your device so maps remain available even without an internet connection.

Offline maps do not contain your personal information.

Information We Collect

Path Keeper accesses your precise location only while you are actively using the tracking feature.

Your location is used to record routes, display your current position, and support offline navigation.

Location information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Permissions We Request

Location Permission: Required to display your current position, record your route, and save tracking history.

Background Location: Required to continue recording your route when the screen is locked or the application is running in the background.

Notification Permission: Required to display the foreground service notification while tracking is active.

Battery Optimization: Optional. Disabling battery optimization can improve long distance tracking reliability on some Android devices.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to record tracking sessions, display maps and route history, improve application reliability, fix bugs, and provide offline map functionality.

We do not use your information for advertising purposes.

Data Storage

Path Keeper stores route history, downloaded offline maps, and application settings locally on your device.

No tracking history is uploaded to our servers.

Future versions may introduce optional cloud synchronization. If that feature becomes available, this Privacy Policy will be updated before it is released.

Data Sharing

Path Keeper does not sell your personal information.

We do not share your location or tracking history with advertisers.

Information may only be shared when required by applicable law.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

However, no storage system or internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Your Choices

You can stop tracking at any time.

You can revoke permissions from your device settings.

You can delete downloaded offline maps.

You can uninstall the application to remove locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

Path Keeper can be used by people of all ages. If you are under the age required by your local laws to provide consent, you should use the application with the permission or supervision of a parent or legal guardian.

Path Keeper does not knowingly collect or store personal information from users on our servers.

Advertising

Future versions of Path Keeper may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob.

Advertising services may collect device identifiers or other information according to their own Privacy Policy.

Path Keeper does not share your location history with advertising providers.

Third Party Services

Path Keeper uses third party services to provide maps and other application features.

Future versions may integrate additional services such as advertising or optional cloud synchronization.

Each third party service is governed by its own Privacy Policy.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

When changes are made, the Last Updated date will be revised.
